Walk through an inspection

The inspection, reimagined.

Scroll to see how Truvis guides you through a typical car viewing. Six scenes. One sticky phone. Each scene is a real moment — and a feature you'll use when it counts.

Step 01
🔍

You arrive at the car. Truvis already knows what breaks on this model.

Before you touch anything, you get the known weak points, common failures, and what deserves a second look — for this exact model, year, and engine. You start informed instead of intimidated.

Step 02
🚦

You start looking. Truvis tells you what's cosmetic and what kills the deal.

Every finding gets a severity score. Green means relax. Yellow means ask follow-up questions. Red means this is serious. No guessing, no Googling at the car.

Step 03
💲

You check your budget. A worn timing belt matters more when €1,200 isn't spare change.

Truvis weighs every finding against your financial reality. The same issue can be a shrug for one buyer and a dealbreaker for another. Your budget shapes the verdict.

Step 04
😎

The seller is watching. Your screen shows a neutral checklist. They see thorough — you see the truth.

Discreet mode hides severity scores, red flags, and warnings from view. The seller sees a buyer being careful. Not alarmed. Not ready to walk away. Not yet.

Step 05
✋

Something's wrong. Truvis tells you to walk away when you can't tell yourself.

When a finding crosses the line from "negotiate" to "leave," the app makes it unambiguous. No emotion, just logic. Because the hardest part of a bad deal is admitting it to yourself.

Step 06
📈

You're ready to talk price. Every finding documented. Fair price range calculated.

You hand the seller evidence, not emotions. Cosmetic issues, real repair costs, and talking points — all in a clean report. You leave with leverage.
